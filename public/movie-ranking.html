Jurassic Park

This movie holds an extraordinary place in my heart because it was the first movie that I saw in theaters at the age of 9. I am not a huge dinosaur enthusiast but the movie stands the test of time for children of all ages. I know when I need a quick pick me up, this movie never fails.

Moulin Rouge

Now hear me out! This one is a hard sell but the cast is amazing with Nicole Kidman and Ewan McGregor. Discribed as a "jukebox musical romance drama" and I think I would agree. Romance is not usually at the top of my watch list, but the artisty of the cinematography is what won me over. And Ewan McGregor singing! If you love Shakespearan style comedies, you will love it too.

Anything Star Wars

I just can't quit this. Created by George Lucas this American Space Opera (that is just funny to day) has expanded into various film, television, video games, novels, comic books and SO MUCH MORE! The themes are timeless, the science is less strict then traditional physics and they have laser swords! What more could you really need?
