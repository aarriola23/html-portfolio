The Best Movies According to Amanda

My top 3 movies of all time.

Jurassic Park

This movie holds an extraordinary place in my heart because it was the first movie that I saw in theaters at the age of 9. I am not a huge dinosaur enthusiast but the movie stands the test of time and children of all ages love it.

Moulin Rouge

Set in the 1890s during the French Art Revolution, this musical is both a love story and an experience of art, music, and culture from a time long forgotten. Understanding the undertones of the movie and the struggles of the people in this period only makes the journey that much better.

Anything Star Wars

Offering a healthy dose of Sci-Fi and culture, these films are cult classics. It is a lesson in the idea of totalitarian rule and human nature to rebel against oppression. I am a huge fan of the story and watch everything related to them. There are some arguments of cannon vs. non-cannon material but I enjoy sharing my passion for the library of material and engaging new fans in exploring the universe created by Lucas.
