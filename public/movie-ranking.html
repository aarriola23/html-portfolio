The Best Movies According to Amanda

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Jurassic Park

This movie holds a very special place in my heart because it was the first movie that I saw in theaters at the age of 9. The movie itself still stands the test of time and children of all ages love it.

Moulin Rouge

Set in the 1890's during the French Art Revolution, this musical is both a love story and an experience into art, musicals and culture for a time long ago.

Anything Star Wars

Offering a health dose of Sci-Fi and culture, these films are cult classics. I am a huge fan of the story and watch everything related to them. I enjoy sharing my passion for the library of material and engaging new fans.
